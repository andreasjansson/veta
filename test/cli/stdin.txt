=====
reinitialize and add note with body from stdin
=====
veta init --reinitialize
echo "Content from stdin" | veta add --title "Stdin note" --tags "stdin,test"
-----
Reinitialized veta database in .veta/db.sqlite
Added note 1

=====
verify stdin content was saved
=====
veta show 1
-----
